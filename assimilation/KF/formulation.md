# Basic equation in Lorenz (1996)
\begin{equation}
\dfrac{dX_k}{dt} =\left(X_{k+1}-X_{k-2} \right) X_{k-1}-X_k+F,\quad (k=1,\cdots ,N).
\label{eq:exam-3-2-3-1}
\end{equation}
\begin{equation}
X_{k-N}=X_k=X_{k+N}.
\label{eq:exam-3-2-3-2}
\end{equation}
